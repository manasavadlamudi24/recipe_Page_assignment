Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a skillet, cook diced pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  4. While the spaghetti is still hot, quickly toss it with the egg and cheese mixture until well-coated. The residual heat will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little pasta water gradually until desired consistency is reached.
  5. Stir in the cooked pancetta or bacon. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley if desired. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
